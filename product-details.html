🌟 Product Description

Indulge in the rich, golden sweetness of Alphonso Mangoes, India’s most loved and globally celebrated variety. Grown in the sun-kissed orchards of Ratnagiri and Devgad, these mangoes are handpicked at peak ripeness to deliver unmatched aroma, flavor, and juiciness.

✅ Why You'll Love It

  • Naturally ripened, carbide-free
  • Smooth, fiber less texture
  • Bursting with tropical flavor and fragrance
  • Perfect for eating raw, smoothies, desserts & more
  • Packed with Vitamin A, C, and antioxidants

📦 Storage Tips

  • Store at room temperature until fully ripe
  • Once ripe, refrigerate and consume within 3–5 days
  • Keep unwashed until ready to eat

🍴 Serving Suggestions

  • Slice and enjoy chilled
  • Blend into a creamy mango smoothie
  • Make mango lassi, desserts, or add to fruit salads
